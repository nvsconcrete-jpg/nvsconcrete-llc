About NVS

NVS Concrete LLC specializes in highway paving, structural foundations, flatwork, site improvements, and public infrastructure concrete construction. Our crews support municipal facilities, churches, public projects, and commercial concrete scopes with quality workmanship and dependable coordination.

We work closely with contractors, inspectors, suppliers, and trades to maintain production schedules while meeting project requirements, safety expectations, and finish standards.

Core scopes include highway concrete paving, Jointed Plain Concrete Pavement (JPCP), sidewalks, curb and gutter, drilled piers, foundations, structural pads, flatwork, and site concrete improvements.
